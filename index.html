I am a Ph.D. candidate in Statistics at the Institute of Statistics and Big Data, Renmin University of China, supervised by Prof. Wei Ma. I am currently visiting the MRC Biostatistics Unit, University of Cambridge, hosted by Dr. David Robertson. I am fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Hanzhong Liu (Tsinghua University) and Prof. Feifang Hu (George Washington University).

Prior to this, I received my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from the School of the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China in 2021.

Research Interests

My research focuses on precision medicine and optimization of clinical trials. Specifically, I am interested in:

  • Adaptive Design and Inference in Clinical Trials

  • Sequential Decision Making

  • Foundation Models for Statistics
